This Project analyzing state populations in Nigeria between 2006 and 2019

This PowerBI project focuses on analyzing the population trends of states in Nigeria over the period from 2006 to 2019. The analysis uncovered a significant 43% increase in the population of Nigeria during this timeframe, highlighting the country's demographic shifts and growth. Among the states, Kano, Lagos, Kaduna, Katsina, and Oyo emerged as the top five with the highest population figures. These states likely experienced rapid urbanization, economic growth, and other factors contributing to population increase. On the other hand, Taraba, Bayelsa, Nasarawa, Ebonyi, and the Federal Capital Territory (FCT) were identified as having the least population. Understanding the population dynamics of these states is crucial for various stakeholders, including policymakers, urban planners, and businesses operating in these regions. Overall, this analysis provides valuable insights into the changing demographics of Nigeria. By leveraging PowerBI to visualize and analyze population data, stakeholders can make informed decisions across a wide range of industries and sectors. From infrastructure development to healthcare planning and market targeting, this analysis offers actionable insights that can drive positive change and progress in Nigeria. #DataAnalysis #PopulationTrends.
